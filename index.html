Welcome! My name's Ian, but my mates call me Twitch (my online alter ego). I'm a web developer based in Sydney, Australia.

Q. How did you learn web development? A. I'm a self-taught developer with a love for learning and a passion for technology. I learnt everything off the internet by researching best practices and digging through forums such as Reddit's web development forum to find the latest web technologies and news. Q. Why web development? A. I fell in love with web development when I realised that I could create products at a cafe while eating brunch. That meant that I could create something tangible and useful from anywhere in the world and I find that quite extraordinary. Q. What are your aspirations? A. I would like to work in a small company/startup to gain more experience and be able to learn more about the inner workings of a company. One of my future aspirations is to have my own firm, consulting businesses on how to make their business more efficient and profitable through web technologies and automation. Q. How did you get the name Twitch? A. In high school, I could do the voice of a video game character named Twitch. Because of that and the fact that he was my favourite character, my friends started calling me Twitch. It stuck with me as my nickname and online alias. It is now part of how I promote myself. What's more memorable? Some guy named Ian or Twitch?
